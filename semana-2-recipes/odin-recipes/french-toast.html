French Toast

Description

This fabulous French toast recipe works with many types of bread â€” white, whole wheat, brioche, cinnamon-raisin, Italian, or French! Delicious served hot with butter and maple syrup.

Ingredients

  • 6 thick slices of White Bread
  • 2 Eggs
  • 2/3 Cup of Milk
  • 1 teaspoon of Vanilla Extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of unsalted butterButter

Step by step

  1. Whisk milk, eggs, vanilla, cinnamon and salt together in a bowl
  2. Lightly butter a griddle or skillet and heat over medium-high heat
  3. Dunk bread in the egg mixture
  4. Transfer to the hot skillet and cook until golden
  5. Serve hot
