Hamburger

Description

Thick or thin, made on the grill or stovetop, this is the best and easiest all-purpose recipe for perfect hamburger patties every time! These juicy, delicious homemade hamburgers are ready in less than 30 minutes and are a must-make for your next cookout.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Spices
  • Toppings of your choice

Step By Step

  1. In a large mixing bowl, add in the ground beef, bread crumbs, eggs, sauce, milk and spices
  2. Press the meat down into an even disk, then divide the patty mixture into 6
  3. Set out a baking sheet with wax paper to hold the patties and put them on top of it one at a time
  4. Press them down into your desired thickness
  5. Pre-heat the grill to medium heat, arouund 170°C - 200°C
  6. Grill for 3-4 minutes per side
  7. Slightly grill the bread for the burger
  8. Put your toppings and the patty in any way you like in the bun
  9. Serve hot.
